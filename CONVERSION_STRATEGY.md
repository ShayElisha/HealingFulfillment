## Lead Magnet Strategy

### PDF Guide: "מדריך להתמודדות עם חרדות"

**Content Outline:**
1. מה זה חרדה? (הסבר קצר)
2. סימפטומים נפוצים
3. 5 טכניקות הרגעה מעשיות
4. תרגילי נשימה
5. מתי לפנות לעזרה מקצועית
6. משאבים נוספים

**Design:**
- Clean, professional layout
- Brand colors
- Easy to read (Hebrew, RTL)
- 8-12 pages

**Delivery:**
- Email capture form
- Instant download after submission
- Follow-up email sequence (optional)

**Placement:**
- HomePage sidebar
- Blog posts about anxiety
- AnxietyPage
- Pop-up (soft, not aggressive)

### Implementation Ideas

**Option 1: Simple Form**
```
[Name Input]
[Email Input]
[Download Button]
```

**Option 2: Two-Step**
1. Click "קבל את המדריך"
2. Form appears (modal or inline)
3. Download link sent via email

**Option 3: Direct Download**
- No email required
- Track downloads via analytics
- Less friction, less lead capture

## Booking Flow Optimization

### Reduce Friction
1. **Simple Form**
   - Only essential fields
   - Clear labels
   - Helpful placeholders

2. **Multiple Options**
   - Online form
   - Phone call
   - WhatsApp
   - Email

3. **Clear Expectations**
   - What happens in first session
   - Duration
   - Location options
   - Pricing transparency

### Follow-Up
- Confirmation email
- Reminder 24h before
- Thank you after session

## Follow-Up Sequences

### After Form Submission
1. Immediate: Thank you email
2. 24h: Additional resources
3. 3 days: Check-in (if no response)
4. 7 days: Final follow-up

### After Booking
1. Confirmation email
2. 24h before: Reminder
